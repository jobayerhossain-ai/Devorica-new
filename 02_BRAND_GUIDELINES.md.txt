# CUSTOMER PSYCHOLOGY (Bangladesh)

Before buying,

customers ask:

Can I trust them?

Have they done this before?

Can they finish on time?

Will they reply after payment?

Will my website actually help my business?

Can I contact them easily?

Therefore,

every page should answer these questions before asking for a sale.

# TYPOGRAPHY PERSONALITY

Typography is our strongest branding tool.

Large Headlines.

Comfortable Reading Width.

Editorial spacing.

Readable paragraphs.

Never overcrowd text.

Never center-align long paragraphs.

---

# COLOR PERSONALITY

Monochrome first.

Accent second.

Background dominates.

Whitespace dominates.

Color should guide attention.

Not decorate.
