# DEVORICA CONTENT SYSTEM

Version: 1.0

---

# PURPOSE

Content should build trust.

Not impress.

Every sentence should help business owners make decisions.

---

# WRITING STYLE

Professional

Simple

Human

Honest

Clear

Avoid hype.

Avoid buzzwords.

Avoid AI marketing language.

---

# HEADLINES

Short.

Specific.

Business focused.

Examples

Build Better Websites.

Digital Experiences That Grow Businesses.

Premium Web Design for Modern Companies.

---

# SUBHEADINGS

Support the headline.

Explain value.

Never repeat the headline.

---

# CTA

# SERVICES

Explain

Problem

↓

Solution

↓

Business Benefit

Never list features first.

---

# TESTIMONIALS

Use real names.

Real companies.

Real results.

Avoid fake testimonials.

---

# FAQ

Answer objections.

Price

Timeline

Support

Technology

Ownership

Maintenance

---

# TONE

Confident

Never arrogant.

Friendly

Never casual.

Professional

Never robotic.

---

# SEO

Write naturally.

Avoid keyword stuffing.

Every page should answer one primary search intent.

---

# AI REVIEW

Before publishing ask

Would a business owner understand this?

Would they trust this?

Could this sentence be shorter?

Can clarity improve?
